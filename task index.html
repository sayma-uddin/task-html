My name is Sayma, and I am 20 years old. I come from a loving family. My mother’s name is Johura Begum, and she is a housewife. My father’s name is Mahtab Uddin, and he is a businessman. I have one younger sister named Tanima, who is 15 years old and a student in class 10. Being the elder daughter, I take pride in my family.I studied at Dasura High School and currently attend Beanibazar Government College for my higher studies. My village is Shodorpur, located in the Beanibazar upazila of the Sylhet district. As a proud Bangladeshi, I cherish my roots and values.In my free time, I enjoy gardening, watching movies, and occasionally listening to music. I believe I am a determined and positive person, and I strive to be the best version of myself every day.
